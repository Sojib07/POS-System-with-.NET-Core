In the "Current Date and" Time section, date and time are being automatically generated.

In the "Item Selection" section, you can choose one item at a time to add.
The dropdown options are coming from "Items" table in the database.
As soon as a valid item is selected, the "Quantity" input field, "Add to Bill" button and "Reset" button will be enabled. 
The items per unit price will automaticatlly be there in "Price/Unit" field.

In the "Bill Details" section, the added items are showed in a table as soon as they are added from the "Item Selection" section.
Relevant Sub Total and Grand Total are also automatically updated with additional tax.
Discount can also be added and the Grand total is automatically changed depending on the discount percentage.
